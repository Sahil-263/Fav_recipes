Healthy Greek Yogurt Parfait

Description

This healthy Greek yogurt parfait is a delicious and nutritious way to start your day or enjoy as a snack. It's made with creamy Greek yogurt, fresh fruits, granola, and a drizzle of honey. Perfect for a quick and satisfying treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1 cup mixed fresh fruits (e.g., berries, banana slices, kiwi)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • Optional toppings: nuts, seeds, shredded coconut

Instructions

  1. In a glass or bowl, add a layer of Greek yogurt at the bottom.
  2. Next, add a layer of granola over the yogurt.
  3. Add a layer of mixed fresh fruits on top of the granola.
  4. Repeat the layers until you reach the top of the glass or bowl.
  5. Drizzle honey or maple syrup over the top layer.
  6. Sprinkle optional toppings like nuts, seeds, or shredded coconut if desired.
  7. Enjoy your healthy and delicious Greek yogurt parfait!
